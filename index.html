Tortellini

Tortellini is firmware for the Arduino Leonardo-based BoilerBadge that provides "social motivation". By boasting behavioral data from fitness trackers (for example, the completion of a daily step goal) or a personality metric (IBM Watson's impression of one's niceness, determined by their social media), users will be more inclined to accomplish their goals and improve themselves.

Clock

If no metric is selected, a binary/analog clock is displayed. Four LEDs in the upper-left quadrant show the binary representation of the hour from 0 to 12. The top LED blinks each second. The other eleven LEDs indicate the minutes of the hour (in five minute increments).

Personality Metrics

In the personality metrics mode, each badge (slave) will receive a command from the central badge (master). The command from the master will light the slave's LEDs according to the slave owner's computed personality level, on a scale from 0 - 100 %.
