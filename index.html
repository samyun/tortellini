Tortellini - A badge clock

An Arduino BoilerBadge Clock.
download .ZIPdownload .TGZ

Tortellini is a programmed Arduino based badge that works as a clock. It uses the Boilermake BoilerBadge I.

The BoilerBadge has 16 LEDs arranged evenly in a circle. Four LEDs show the binary representation of the hour from 0 to 12. One LED blinks according to the second. The other eleven LEDs show show minutes in five minute increments.
